# CarbonSense X — Applied AI Research Plan

## Research Question

> **Can AI-driven behavioral optimization identify the smallest lifestyle changes that achieve the greatest carbon reduction while minimizing user resistance?**

---

## Novel Contributions

1. **Carbon DNA System**
   - Classifies users dynamically into specific behavioral carbon persona categories (e.g., *High Flyer*, *Eco Enthusiast*) based on primary category emissions ratios and calculated habit frequencies.

2. **Behavior Intelligence Engine**
   - Implements automated heuristics and sequence analysis on user activity entries to detect carbon-intensive behaviors, identifying critical intervention windows.

3. **Forecast Engine**
   - Models multi-horizon projections (30-day, 90-day, and yearly scenarios) to visualize user footprint trajectories under business-as-usual vs. optimized lifestyle paths.

4. **Intervention Optimization Engine**
   - Formulates recommendation ranking as a multi-objective optimization problem, balancing target carbon savings against user-stated preference constraints, difficulty, and financial thresholds.

5. **Planet Twin Simulation**
   - Establishes a mathematical model linking individual lifestyle metrics to micro-planetary health indicators (PPM, forest cover index, glacier volume indices).
